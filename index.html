THE CLIENT

Jetlite is a young company from Hamburg founded in 2017, which develops personalized lighting solutions tailored to the inner clock to increase humans' health and well-being.

Their clients so far include air carriers, cruise liners and even car manufacturers that see the value of Human-Centric Lighting. Now that they want to enter the consumer market and provide lighting solutions for personal use, they tasked Jetlab with the development of an app that takes their scientific research to users' homes. We were more than happy to get to work.
